# CAMPAIGN_PLAN

Target campaign breadth: `10` districts with `5` stages each, for a `50-stage` campaign.

Current authored status: `10` playable districts and `50` live stages. All ten districts are in-game and every district now meets the `5`-stage campaign target.

1. `King's Road` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): outer farms, pilgrim roads, and bell-tower wards where the Lantern Caravan first meets the Rotbound Host.
2. `Saltwake Docks` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): flooded quays, chain cranes, and wreck piers built around heavier undead and dockside choke points.
3. `Emberforge March` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): smelter rows, railyards, and furnace crowns focused on sapper dives, bone nests, and battlefield hazards.
4. `Ashen Ward` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): purge cloisters, sealed courts, and black-vault checkpoints centered on hexers, heralds, and containment hazards.
5. `Thornwall Pass` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): cliff roads, watch forts, and avalanche shrines that push mountain weather, raid-horn pressure, and vertical kill zones.
6. `Hollow Basilica` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): ruined cathedrals, ossuary plazas, and reliquary vaults where relic guardians and necromantic ceremony define the fights.
7. `Mire of Saints` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): bog causeways, drowned chapels, and plague ferries that slow the march and lean into attrition pressure.
8. `Sunfall Steppe` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): burned waystations, open grassland forts, and roaming siege camps built around fast rider strikes and fire volleys.
9. `Gloamwood Verge` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): thorn groves, witch circles, and haunted timber roads shaped by ambush timing, snares, and curse traps.
10. `Crownfall Citadel` (`Playable`, target `5` stages): bridge forts, breach yards, and the inner keep where earlier threat patterns converge into the capital siege finale.
